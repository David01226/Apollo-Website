Benefits of Solar

Saving you money – Save money on your utility bills and protect your home or business against price hikes by the energy giants.

Environmental Benefits – Solar power enables us to harness the limitless supply of energy provided by the sun every day, allowing us to transition to a more sustainable, carbon neutral way of life.

Low Maintenance – Once a system is installed maintenance is practically nil, allowing you to enjoy the benefits without worrying about ongoing servicing and maintenance costs.

Increases the value of your property – Adding solar to your property will increase your home's Energy Performance Rating. Reduced running costs also make it more attractive to future buyers or tenants.

Government incentives – There are Government incentives in place to encourage homeowners and businesses to go green. For business, it falls under the Annual Investment Allowance: HMRC will allow your business to deduct the entire capital cost of installing a PV Solar System on your premises from your taxable profits, reducing the cost of going green and providing an even faster return on investment.

Energy independence – Battery storage options empower you to take full control of your electricity supply, gaining energy independence and eliminating reliance on large energy suppliers.

Heat Your Own Water – By adding a solar diverter to your PV system, you can make your property even more efficient whilst also reducing your gas bill.

ARE SOLAR PANELS SUITABLE FOR MY BUSINESS?

Most homes and businesses are suitable for Solar Power. However, we conduct on site inspections, assess each property individually and design a bespoke system, tailored for every customer. Some factors to consider are:

Size
Some properties possess larger roof spaces than others. The more panels you can fit, the more energy is produced. It is important, therefore, to compare your energy needs with the number of panels that can be fitted.
Pitch
The typical angle of most UK rooftops is 30-40 degrees. Solar panels can be installed on a range of different pitches and roof types. From flat roof to wall mounted systems, there's always a route to optimising a customer's available space.
Direction
Where possible, panels will be installed on South facing roof spaces. Not all customers have the benefit of South facing roofs, in which case East/West systems are employed.
