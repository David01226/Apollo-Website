WANT TO JOIN THE TEAM?

At Apollo we believe in making changes that will impact the planet positively for future generations. Part of this commitment is making use of the renewable resources that that we have at our disposal. One of the best sources of renewable energy is Solar Energy. At Apollo, we want to make this affordable for everyone whilst offering the best products and tailoring a system that allows individual homeowners, businesses and Governments to meet their energy needs.

If you are interested in joining our team of qualified engineers and professionals and having a career within the renewable energy industry, take a look below to see our current job vacancies.

Solar Panel Installer

We are looking for qualified and trainee Solar Panel installers. The suitable candidate for this role must have a good attitude, work well as part of a team and be comfortable working outdoors and at height.

The candidate will work hands on delivering the solar energy system for the customer and will work with a range of different mounting systems ranging from ground mounted systems to pitched on and in-roof systems.
