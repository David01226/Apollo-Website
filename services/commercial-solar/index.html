ARE SOLAR PANELS SUITABLE FOR MY BUSINESS?

Most homes and businesses are suitable for Solar Power. However, we conduct on site inspections, assess each property individually and design a bespoke system, tailored for every customer. Some factors to consider are:

Size
Some properties possess larger roof spaces than others. The more panels you can fit, the more energy is produced. It is important, therefore, to compare your energy needs with the number of panels that can be fitted.
Pitch
The typical angle of most UK rooftops is 30-40 degrees. Solar panels can be installed on a range of different pitches and roof types. From flat roof to wall mounted systems, there's always a route to optimising a customer's available space.
Direction
Where possible, panels will be installed on South facing roof spaces. Not all customers have the benefit of South facing roofs, in which case East/West systems are employed.
