Roofing

Common Signs Your Roof Needs Attention

Leaks and Water Damage
If you notice water stains on your ceilings or walls, it's a clear sign that your roof is leaking. Active drips inside your home during or after rainfall indicate breaches in your roof.
Damaged or Missing Tiles
Tiles that are cracked, curled, or buckling no longer provide effective protection. Gaps where tiles are missing expose your roof to water damage and other issues.
Sagging Roof
Any part of your roof that appears to droop or sag is a serious concern. This could indicate structural issues or extensive water damage that require professional evaluation and repair.
