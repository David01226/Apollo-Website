About Apollo

At Apollo Renewable Installations, we are passionate about making a positive change in the world, harnessing renewable resources to help people power their lives.

Our in-house professionals have a wealth of knowledge & experience in the renewable energy sector. Our goal is to assist customers in achieving a more cost efficient and sustainable future by reducing their energy bills and carbon footprint.

We offer a comprehensive range of services and pride ourselves on making Solar Power more accessible to homeowners and businesses across the country.
